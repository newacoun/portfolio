OVERVIEW - PRESIDIO SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

Presidio Solutions Private Limited is an unlisted private company incorporated on 27 November, 2015. It is classified as a private limited company and is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It's authorized share capital is INR 15.00 lac and the total paid-up capital is INR 10.00 lac.

Presidio Solutions Private Limited's operating revenues range is INR 100 cr - 500 cr for the financial year ending on 31 March, 2023. It's EBITDA has increased by 17.24 % over the previous year. At the same time, it's book networth has increased by 149.21 %. Other performance and liquidity ratios are available here.

The current status of Presidio Solutions Private Limited is - Active.

The last reported AGM (Annual General Meeting) of Presidio Solutions Private Limited, per our records, was held on 30 August, 2023.

Presidio Solutions Private Limited has three directors - Pandurangan Gopinathan, Robert Michael Cagnazzi, and others.

The Corporate Identification Number (CIN) of Presidio Solutions Private Limited is U74999TN2015PTC103124. The registered office of Presidio Solutions Private Limited is at Level 7 & 8, Door No. 51, IndiQube Alpline Jawahar Nehru Roa d, Block No. 4, SIDCO Industrial Estate, Chennai City Corporation, Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
